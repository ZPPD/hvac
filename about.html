About RJD Heating and Cooling

Our Story

All of our staff are trained and educated on the most up-to-date, innovative and cutting-edge HVAC systems. RJD Heating and Cooling takes great pride in providing quality workmanship and service with competitive prices. Our knowledge and experience speaks for itself.

RJD Heating and Cooling offers service and scheduled maintenance to our commercial and residential customers. Our technicians uphold the highest standards of efficiency, attention to detail and use only quality parts and equipment.

Our company provides a full spectrum of work of any level of complexity. Our team consists of highly qualified professionals. We have gained extensive experience while solving the most complex technical and organizational tasks, what allows us to provide you with the best service.

Company History

In 2013, Robert Dougherty fouded RJD Heating and Cooling, combining many years of experience in the mechanical fields. RJD Heating and Cooling provides design, sales, service and installation for both residential and commercial. The day to day communication between our teams allows for a smooth running project with ultimately leads to a superior end product.

Our Mission

It is our first priority here at RJD Heating and Cooling to provide the highest level of professionalism, integrity and honesty throughout your project, whether it be new construction, re-construction, heating or air conditioning repair. Every effort is made to ensure the longevity of our company through repeat and referral business, achieved by customer satisfaction. We strive to create a lasting relationship with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of the RJD team.

Rober Dougherty, Owner and Operator
